About Stargazer

Stargazer is a project that started out as a collaborative exercise between Creative Technology and Electrical Engineering students at the University of Twente. After the prototype was finished and the exercise was over, the project was further improved and extended. The (messy) source code of this project is available on GitHub.

Originally developed in 2015 by Jonathan Juursema, Peter Verzijl, Guido Kuijper and Remi Jonkman at the University of Twente.
